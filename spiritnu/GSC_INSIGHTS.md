# GSC Data → What to Optimize (spiritnu/)

Use this data to **decide what to optimize**: queries and pages that need better titles, links, or content so you get more clicks from the impressions you already have.

- **Chart.csv** (time series): When traffic happens (UTC+3). Use to optimize timing (e.g. when to highlight “today’s number” or run tests).
- **Queries.csv**: What people search; which get **clicks** vs. **impressions only**. Use to optimize: titles, meta descriptions, homepage copy, and internal links for high-impression, low-CTR terms.
- **Pages.csv**: Which URLs get impressions and clicks. Use to optimize: underperforming pages (high impressions, 0 clicks) and to reinforce top performers.

---

## Chart.csv – Time

- **Peak traffic**: ~14:00–22:00 UTC+3 (afternoon/evening).
- **Position**: ~9–11; **CTR**: ~0.2–0.9%.
- **Action**: Consider “Today’s number” or time-based messaging in peak hours if you add dynamic content later.

## Queries.csv – What to act on

### High CTR when we rank (double down)

- “466 meaning” (33% CTR), “1737 meaning in love” (100%), “angel number meaning” variants, “meaning in love”, “twin flame”, “money”.
- **Action**: Keep and strengthen angel number + meaning + love/twin flame/money content; ensure these themes appear in titles and on the homepage.

### High impressions, 0 clicks (CTR opportunity)

- Examples: “665 meaning” (90 imp), “183 meaning” (72), “2015 meaning” (54), “angel number 1321 meaning” (51), “579 meaning” (23), “774 meaning” (26), “480 meaning” (26).
- **Action**: Improve title/meta for these pages; surface “Trending” or “Often searched” numbers (e.g. 665, 183, 579, 774, 480) on homepage or meaning hub to capture intent.

### Query themes to support on homepage

- **Meaning** (generic + “in love”, “love”, “twin flame”, “money”, “career”).
- **Action**: Category tabs or links: Meaning, Twin Flame, Love, Money, Career, Soulmate; search bar for “number or topic”; popular/trending numbers.

## Recommended homepage actions (done or to keep)

1. **Hero**: Headline + subheading + one primary CTA (Calculate) + category tabs (Meaning, Twin Flame, Love, Money, Career, Soulmate) + search bar + Popular numbers.
2. **Search**: Redirect to `/meaning/angel-number/[number]` when input is a number; otherwise `/meaning?q=...` or search page.
3. **Popular / Trending**: Show 111–888; optionally add GSC high-impression numbers (e.g. 665, 183, 579, 774).
4. **Tools section**: Compare Numbers, Number Quiz, Famous Life Paths, Numerology Blog – clear cards, one accent.
5. **Engagement**: Today’s Angel Number, Moon phase, Affirmation, Love Compatibility – light cards, no dark boxes.
6. **Copy**: “Personalized insights based on your birth date” to match search intent and differentiator.

All of the above keep a **clean light theme** (light background, dark text, single amber accent) to protect readability and CTR.

## Summary: Use GSC data to know what to optimize

| Data | Use it to optimize |
|------|---------------------|
| **Queries with high impressions, 0 clicks** | Titles, meta descriptions, and first paragraph so the snippet is more compelling; add internal links from homepage/meaning hub. |
| **Queries with good CTR** | Double down: keep or strengthen that content and link to it from homepage/tools. |
| **Pages with high impressions, 0 clicks** | Title and meta; consider a “Trending” or “Often searched” block that links to those URLs. |
| **Chart (time)** | When to run tests or when to emphasize “today’s number” if you add time-based content. |
