# Monetization Strategy: Research & Recommendations

**Your traffic:** ~25k impressions/day, ~100 clicks/day  
**Current:** PDFs (Lemon Squeezy $17–$49) + ClickBank affiliates — **not converting.**

This doc summarizes how other numerology/spirituality sites monetize and what to do next.

---

## 1. How Other Numerology & Spirituality Sites Monetize

| Method | How it works | Typical in niche |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Display ads** | AdSense, Ezoic, Media.net — revenue per impression/view | Very common; passive income |
| **Psychic/reading affiliates** | Promote live psychic/tarot/astrology services; $100–$150 per paying customer | High-ticket, experience-based |
| **Numerology-specific affiliates** | Numerologist PRO, Numerology Toolbox (30% on €32–€1,150) | Mid–high ticket |
| **Own digital products** | PDFs, reports, courses, consultations | You already do PDFs |
| **Lead magnets + email** | Free daily number / chart → email capture → nurture → sell | Top sites use “free daily number by email” |
| **ClickBank / info products** | VSLs, ebooks, audio (Moon Reading, Soulmate Sketch, etc.) | Common but often low conversion in spirituality |

**Why PDFs and ClickBank often underperform in this niche**

- Spirituality visitors often want **experience** (a reading, personal insight) more than **information** (a PDF).
- ClickBank VSLs can feel salesy; trust is lower than with a “try 3 free minutes with a psychic” CTA.
- Too much friction before checkout (many required fields) can kill conversions.
- One high-ticket psychic sale ($100–$150) can beat many small PDF/ClickBank sales.

## 2. Recommended Monetization Mix (In Order of Impact)

### A. Add display ads (highest impact for your traffic)

- **Why:** 25k impressions/day ≈ **750k pageviews/month**. You get paid on **impressions**, not conversions.
- **Spirituality niche RPM:** roughly **$2–$15** (lower than finance/tech, but still meaningful).
- **Ballpark:** $2–5 RPM → **~$1,500–$3,750/month**; $5–10 RPM → **~$3,750–$7,500/month**.
- **Action:**  
  - Apply for **Google AdSense** (numerology/angel number sites can get approved; e.g. AngleNumber.net is sold as “Perfect Google Adsense niche”).  
  - If AdSense is slow or rejected, try **Ezoic** or **Media.net** (contextual display).  
- Put ad units in content (after first paragraph, mid-article, end of article). Don’t overload; keep UX readable.

**This should be your first priority** — it turns existing impressions into predictable revenue with no conversion required.

---

### B. Add high-ticket psychic/reading affiliates (replace or complement ClickBank)

- **Programs:**  
  - **PsychicOz** — ~$150 per paying customer, first 3 minutes free (good for conversion).  
  - **Kasamba** — ~$125; **Keen** — ~$100; **Psychic Source** — ~$100.  
- **Why they convert better:** “Get a live reading” matches the intent of people searching for angel numbers/numerology; one sale = $100–$150 vs many $17 PDFs or low-converting ClickBank.
- **How to use:**  
  - Soft CTA: e.g. “Want a deeper, personal reading? Try 3 free minutes with a psychic.”  
  - Place on meaning pages, love/twin flame, compatibility, and in the footer or a dedicated “Readings” section.  
  - Optionally in **daily number emails**: “Today’s number suggests [X]. For a personalized reading, try 3 free minutes here.”
- **Action:** Sign up for PsychicOz (and optionally 1–2 others), add one clear CTA per page type (not 5 different affiliates). Track clicks and, if the program offers it, conversions.

---

### C. Keep PDFs but reduce friction and add a lead-magnet path

- **Problem:** Requiring focus, feeling, time horizon, etc. before “Get your PDF” can reduce completions.
- **Ideas:**  
  - Add “Personalization optional” so users can checkout with just email + number.  
  - Consider one **low-friction product** (e.g. $7 “Quick Number Report” or “Your number in 60 seconds”) to get first purchase, then upsell blueprint/relationship.  
- **Lead magnet:** You already have “free daily number by email.” Use it as the main funnel: **free value → email → nurture (2–3 emails with real insight) → then** offer PDF or psychic affiliate. Don’t lead with “Buy PDF” or ClickBank on first touch.

---

### D. Use email to monetize, not just to send the daily number

- Send the **daily number** (you already have the cron).  
- Every few emails, add **one** clear CTA: either your PDF or “Try 3 free minutes with a psychic” (affiliate link).  
- Keep copy helpful; soft sell: “If you want to go deeper, here’s a way to get a personal reading.”

---

### E. ClickBank: keep only the best fits or demote them

- If Moon Reading, Soulmate Sketch, Numerologist VSL, etc. are not converting, **don’t rely on them as primary**.  
- Options:  
  - **Reduce presence:** one slot per page (e.g. after main CTA) or only on high-intent pages.  
  - **A/B test:** replace one ClickBank block with a psychic affiliate CTA and compare click-through and (if trackable) revenue.  
- Prioritize **psychic affiliates + display ads + your PDFs** over stacking many ClickBank promos.

## 4. Summary

- **Display ads** are the fastest way to monetize 25k impressions/day; spirituality RPM is modest but meaningful — implement first.  
- **Psychic/reading affiliates** ($100–$150/sale) fit the “I want a reading” intent better than generic ClickBank products; add one and feature it clearly.  
- **PDFs** are worth keeping; reduce friction and support them with email nurture (daily number → value → offer).  
- **ClickBank**: keep only where it fits; demote or replace with psychic affiliate where it doesn’t convert.
